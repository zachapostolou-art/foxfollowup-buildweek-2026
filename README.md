# FoxFollowUp — OpenAI Build Week 2026

FoxFollowUp is a human-first pharmacy follow-up application created for OpenAI Build Week 2026.

## The problem

The pharmacist’s care often ends when the patient leaves the pharmacy. Important follow-up information, progress updates and communication may then become fragmented or lost.

## The solution

FoxFollowUp helps pharmacists continue supporting people after their visit through a simple, structured and personal follow-up experience.

Its philosophy is:

> Care does not end when a person leaves our pharmacy. That is when it begins.

## Core features

- Pharmacist-to-member follow-up chat
- Treatment and wellness progress tracking
- Visual timeline with milestones and photos
- Quick progress reactions: better, the same or worse
- Personal goals and pharmacist-defined protocols
- Private pharmacist notes
- Voice and photo support
- Greek and English interface
- Clear emergency-care disclaimer

## Human-first approach

FoxFollowUp is designed to reduce cognitive load for both the pharmacist and the member. It supports continuity of care and encourages appropriate communication without replacing the physician or emergency medical services.

## How GPT-5.6 and Codex were used

GPT-5.6 supported the product strategy, healthcare workflow design, feature prioritization, bilingual content and UX decisions.

Codex helped transform the concept into a working interactive prototype, refine the application structure and support the Build Week submission workflow.

The project was developed through iterative collaboration: defining a real pharmacy problem, testing user flows and converting professional pharmacy experience into a usable digital product.

## Safety notice

FoxFollowUp is not intended for emergencies, diagnosis or replacement of medical care. In an emergency, users must contact the appropriate emergency medical services.
